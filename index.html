I am a Professor of Data Science at the Department of Statistics, LSE, and a member of the Data Science group. My research interest is statistical machine learning with focus on information theory (ITE), kernel methods, scalable computation, and their applications. These applications include shape-constrained prediction, hypothesis testing, safety-critical learning, style transfer, distribution regression, dictionary learning, structured sparsity, independent subspace analysis and its extensions, Bayesian inference, finance, economics, analysis of climate data, criminal data analysis, collaborative filtering, emotion recognition, face tracking, remote sensing, natural language processing, and gene analysis.
